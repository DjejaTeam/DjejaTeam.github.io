HOW IT WORKS

A compact under-coop system collects manure, feeds it into an anaerobic digester, and channels biogas to nearby homes. The digestate becomes safe fertilizer for local fields.

1

Smart collection

Scraper channels and pits keep barns tidy and feed the digester automatically.

2

Efficient digestion

Temperature‑stable tanks maximize methane yield with low maintenance.

3

Last‑mile energy

Biogas is piped or bottled for cooking; generators can supply micro‑grids.
